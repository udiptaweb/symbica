Who we are

Born from a dream of empowerment.

Symbica started with a dream of empowering specially-abled people by bringing them back into mainstream life — where they feel confident, self-reliant and full of self-esteem.

We believe assistive technology should never feel like a compromise. It should feel like a part of you — an extension of your intent, your ability and your identity.

🤝

Every decision starts with the person who will wear our technology every single day.

What we're building

A smart bionic hand that feels like your own.

Our current focus: a bionic hand engineered to generate a genuine sense of ownership — comfortable and confident to operate in everyday life.

Affordable

Built to last with durable materials and a long battery life — no constantly replacing parts, no running out of power when you need it most.

Sweatproof

Tested in a variety of conditions to withstand whatever you throw at it. No matter how much you sweat, our bionic hand keeps working for you.

Custom Skin

Everyone's skin is unique. Choose from a range of options — a tone that matches your natural color, or a bright, vibrant color that stands out.

5 Finger Motion

Precise and responsive five-finger motion keeps you confident and in control of every movement — complete tasks with ease, like a natural hand.

Smart Auto Grasp

Senses the shape and size of objects and adjusts the grip for you — from pencils to water bottles. It's like your own hand, only smarter.
